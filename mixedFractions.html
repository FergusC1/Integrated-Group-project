YEAR 5 FRACTIONS

What is a mixed Fraction?

A mixed fraction is one integer and one proper fraction making up a whole number to write a mixed fraction you need to look write down the whole number and the fraction next to it.

Lets look at an example!

1 is the integer and 3/4 is the proper fraction

What is an Improper Fraction?

An improper fraction is a fraction where the numerator is greater then the denominator

Lets look at an example!

7 is greater then 4 thus making this an improper fraction

Turning an improper fraction into a mixed fraction

To change an improper fraction to a mixed number, follow these steps:

  1. Divide the numerator by the denominator
  2. Write down the whole number part of the answer
  3. Take the remainder and write it over the original denominator as the new numerator
