YEAR 6 FRACTIONS - Adding with Improper Fraction

How to add with improper fractions

Adding with mixed and improper fractions is easily broken down into a few simple steps

  1. The first thing we have to do is convert the mixed fraction into an improper fraction

    2. If you dont know how to convert a mixed fraction into an improper fraction go to our year 5 tutorial section to learn. Year 5 - mixed and improper fractions

  2. Make the denominators the same
  3. Add the numerators together
  4. Convert into a mixed number
